His obsession with the sport leads him into some very funny and exaggerated situations. He’s the kind of character who can turn any conversation into a discussion about soccer, even when it's completely unrelated to the topic at hand. His dedication to the game and his team makes him a comedic figure, often clashing with the more grounded or logical characters around him, like his brother Semsem and others.

Whether he's giving grand speeches about his supposed greatness or diving headfirst into strange plans, Prince adds a unique layer of comedy to the series. Despite his exaggerated sense of self, Prince is also a likable character who forms connections with the other main characters in the show, especially Reda and his family.
